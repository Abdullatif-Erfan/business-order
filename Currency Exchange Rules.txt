ROLES
Base Currency (AFN): No conversion is needed for the base currency.
Greater Currency (USD): For greater currencies, multiply the amount by the exchange rate.
Smaller Currency (PKR, IR): For smaller currencies, divide the amount by the exchange rate.
Zero Total Talabat: If the total talabat for a currency is zero, skip the conversion process and return
